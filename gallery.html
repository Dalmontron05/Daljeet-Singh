Siege-O-Namics

It's a website with advanced tips for operators in the video game Tom Clancy's: Rainbow Six Siege. Each operator has a page dedicated to them. There are plenty of beginner guides and esports-level strategies available online. However, there isn't as much content for intermediate players (which is what the majority of the playerbase is at skill-wise).

Siege has a steep learning curve. If you want to have fun competitively, learning how the operators work on a deeper level can help you use or face against them.
